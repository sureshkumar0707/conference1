About the Conference

The International Conference on Advanced Data-Driven Intelligence and Engineering is a prestigious event dedicated to exploring cutting-edge advancements in data-driven intelligence and engineering fields. The conference serves as a platform for researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and students to exchange knowledge, present their research findings, and discuss emerging trends and challenges in the field. With a focus on advanced data analytics, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and engineering applications, the conference aim to foster collaboration, encourage innovation, and drive the development of solutions that leverage data-driven approaches to address complex problems.

Join us for this impactful conference and be part of the global community shaping the future of data-driven intelligence and engineering.

About the University

Jain University, located in Bangalore, is a renowned educational institution known for its excellence in higher education. With a strong emphasis on holistic development and innovative teaching methods, Jain University offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across varous disciplines. The university is known for its state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and industry-relevant curriculum. It provides students with ample opportunities for research, internships, and industry collaborations, ensuring they are well-prepared for their future careers. Jain University is committed to nurturing talent, fostering creativity, and shaping leaders of tomorrow.
